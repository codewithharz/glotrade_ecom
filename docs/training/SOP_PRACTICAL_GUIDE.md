Trade cycles require initialization and profit processing.

### How to Create a New Trade Cycle:
1. Navigate to **GDIP** -> **Clusters (GDCs)**.
2. Find a cluster with status **"Ready"** (10/10 TPIAs filled).
3. Click **Create Cycle**.
4. **Fill the Form**: Select the **Commodity Type** (Rice, Sugar, etc.). 
5. Click **Schedule Cycle**.

### How to Approve a Credit Request:
1. Navigate to **Credit Requests**.
2. **Review**: Check the user's past spending and the business reason.
3. **Action**: Approve the full requested amount or set a custom limit.

### How to Process a Withdrawal:
1. Navigate to **Withdrawals**.
2. **Action**: Approve if bank transfer is confirmed, or Reject with a reason (e.g., "Invalid Bank Details").
