## üè¢ **Company Overview**

**AfriTrade Hub** is a comprehensive, production-ready e-commerce marketplace platform that connects African buyers and sellers across Nigeria and West African countries. We provide a complete end-to-end solution for online commerce with real-time features, comprehensive admin oversight, and seamless payment processing.

### **Mission Statement**
To create Africa's leading decentralized marketplace, fostering economic growth and cross-border trade through blockchain technology while empowering African merchants and consumers with a secure, efficient, and accessible digital marketplace.

### **Vision**
Starting from Nigeria and expanding across the continent, we aim to become the go-to platform for e-commerce in Africa, providing both traditional and Web3-enabled commerce solutions.
